Hello and welcome to my portfolio site! My name is John Kirch and I’m a web designer/developer living in Ringgold, GA. I’ve been a programmer and developer for 6 years and have worked on projects ranging from basic websites to full blown inventory management systems and ecommerce stores for small to large companies. I work with clients from concept to deployment and everything in between.


I first got into programming when a need arose at a pharmaceutical company where I worked as a maintenance technician. Once I started, I was hooked! After a few months of learning Java I began to build my first inventory management system for the company. It turned out great, but I wanted it to be even better. I began to study web technology and realized that the possibilities were endless. I spent a couple of years learning the basics; from html, css, and javascript, to react, redux, nodejs, express, databases, algorithms, etc. You get the picture, there are never ending technologies and they are constantly being updated and given more utility. This is one of my favorite aspects of coding, the fact that you are always learning. This is a very exciting space to be in and I’m glad to be a part of it!


A little about me personally, I love the outdoors! I am living on a horse farm where it is nice and quiet and I have plenty of time to focus on work. In my spare time I try to get into the mountains with my pup Percy or make it down to Atlanta from time to time to visit family. I love sailing and look forward to retiring one day on a sailboat in the Caribbean and hopefully one day sailing around the world. I am a big believer in cryptocurrencies and the underlying blockchain technology. I have a personal NFT project that I am developing with web3 technologies and find a lot of enjoyment in building an idea from the ground up.
